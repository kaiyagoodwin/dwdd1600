History of the Sneaker

Pursuit of Comfort

  1. Sneakers have moved from the niche to become coveted as fashionable objects. Footwear is now the biggest selling category in the online luxury market and sneakers have made a significant contribution to this growth.
  2. The last decade has seen a huge shift in how sneakers are worn. The pursuit of comfort grew more during the pandemic in 2020, as lockdowns made people further prioritise comfort.
