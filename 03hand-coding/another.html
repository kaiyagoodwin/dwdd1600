History of the Sneaker

Pursuit of Comfort

  1. Sneakers have moved from the niche to become coveted as fashionable objects. Footwear is now the biggest selling category in the online luxury market and sneakers have made a significant contribution to this growth.
  2. The last decade has seen a huge shift in how sneakers are worn. The pursuit of comfort grew more during the pandemic in 2020, as lockdowns made people further prioritise comfort.
  3. Sneakers are designed to be comfortable. Unlike a leather fress shoe, sneakers are designed for maximum flexiblity and comfort.

Why the sneaker is so popular

Reason for Fame
They are incredibly comfortable and can be worn for various activites, from running to walkinga round town. They are very versatile and that allows for them to be worn with multiple outfits. And lastly they are relatively affordable, making them accessible to many people.
Culture
Sneakers have become a popular part of sneaker culture, which is a subculture that is devoted to the collecting and trading of sneakers. smeaker culture had become a global phenomenon, with sneaker enthusiasts worldwide gathering to discuss, trade, and buy the latest releases.
Sneaker Evolution
The evolution of the design has also driven the popularity of sneakers. From introducing new materials and technologies to including features such as air cushioning and arch support.
Conclusion
Sneakers have become incredibly popular due to their comfort, style, and affordability. They are seen everywhere, from the streets to the red carpet, and are even included in the collections of high-end fashion designers. Sneakers have also become a pupilar part of sneaker culture and have evolved sifnificantly over the years.
